A collection of ready-to-use component variants that you can easily copy, paste,
and customize for React and Next.js apps.
Stay tuned for more components, snippets, complete blocks, and design resources coming your way!

## Motivation

We know how challenging it can be to keep your design consistent while meeting the diverse needs of modern websites.
While many libraries promise both, they often come with complex installation processes that get in the way.


<br/>
At PaceUI, we focus on making design easy.
Our components are beautifully crafted, ready to copy, paste, and customize, so you can keep your projects consistent and stylish without the extra work.

<br/>
It’s inspired by shadcn’s approach, and we really love how flexible it is. We’re big fans of what it brings to the table!
